Old Fashioned

Description

Ah, the Old Fashioned... probably my most consumed cocktail. It's a drink that, in my opinion, is suitable for any and all occasions that require a drink in hand.

But enough about me. Let's spread the good news and show you how to make your own.

Ingredients

  • 2oz Whiskey (again, whatever kind you fancy, really)
  • Pinch of Sugar
  • Splash of Water
  • 2 Dashes of Bitters (really can't go wrong with Angostura)
  • 2 Maraschino Cherries
  • A Orange Peel

Steps

  1. In your glass, combine your sugar and water and bitters. Muddles until sugar is dissolved
  2. Add your ice to your cup (I like using single, large cubes or spheres. something like this)
  3. Add Whiskey and stir in a cirvular motion until chilled
  4. Roll and squeeze your orange peel to bring the juices out
  5. Rub the orange peel around the rim of the glass and then add the peel and cherries as a garnish
